Section 1

Applied Mathematics in CS

In Section 11, we moved from the "Why" of math to the "How" of engineering:

  • **Systems:** Reaching consensus with Paxos and Raft.
  • **Databases:** Relational Algebra as set theory.
  • **Graphics:** Quaternions for rotation and Projection for depth.
  • **Finance:** The Black-Scholes PDE for pricing risk.
  • **Strategy:** Nash Equilibrium and the Prisoner's Dilemma.
Section 5

Capstone: The Multi-Agent Bank

Your capstone project for this section is a thought experiment: **Design a Distributed Financial Exchange.**

How do you use **Paxos** to ensure all nodes agree on the price? How do you use **Black-Scholes** to price the contracts? How do you use **Game Theory** to ensure traders don't cheat the auction? Write a high-level architecture diagram and logic flow.
