Section Summary

This section took us from the abstract definition of **Vector Spaces** to the highly practical applications of **Dimension Reduction** and **Mathematical Optimization**.

Linear Structure

Basis, Dimension, Rank, and the Four Fundamental Subspaces definining where data "lives".

Spectral Theory

Eigenvalues, Diagonalization, and SVD showing us the underlying axes of any transformation.

Optimization

Moving from solving fixed equations ($Ax=b$) to minimizing errors ($||Ax-b||$) and general functions via Gradient Descent.

Data Science

PCA and Least Squares proving that Linear Algebra is the bedrock of modern AI.

02

Concept Roadmap

How everything fits together:

  • Orthogonality leads to SVD and Least Squares.
  • Differentiability (Gradients/Hessians) leads to the search for the Global Minimum.
  • Decomposition (PDP⁻¹, UΣVᵀ) allows us to simplify massive operations into scalar ones.
